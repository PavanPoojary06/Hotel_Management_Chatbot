About Udupi Grand

Nestled near the serene Malpe beach, Hotel Udupi Grand is an oasis of comfort and elegance. We pride ourselves on blending traditional Udupi hospitality with modern luxury to create an unforgettable experience for our guests.

From our meticulously designed rooms to our world-class dining, every detail is crafted for your comfort. Whether you're here for business, a pilgrimage, or a relaxing getaway, we are dedicated to making your stay exceptional.
